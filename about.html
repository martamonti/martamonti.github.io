I was born and raised in the suburbs of Milan, I attended Politecnico di Milano, Faculty of Design, where I graduated in Product Design in 2019. I decided to continue my studies at Politecnico with a Master in Communication Design, which I am currently attending. I am interested in design and it fascinates me in the moment that it responds, or at least tries to respond, to real and concrete human needs. What attracts me to an object, an artifact or a system is to ask myself why something was conceived, designed and engineered in a certain way, what the designer wanted to answer in that way. If the artifact in question is intended for children, I am even more interested. To a child, who knows the world day in and day out, everything communicates: the shape, the color, any aspect of any artifact communicates. This is precisely why, after earning a bachelor's degree in industrial product design, I furthered my studies in communication design.
