Peggy

  • Area: Graphic
  • Year: 2019
A new bottle to celebrate women in Venice

The project that was created for Cielo&Terra, a wine company from Vicenza, wants to propose itself as a celebration to women in particular on the Venetian territory. For this reason it could not but be dedicated to Peggy Guggenheim, a great woman very fond of the city of Venice. The artwork was awarded the first position and was taken by the winery as a new bottle to contain rose wine.
