Kids Design

Beetween

  • Area: Product
  • Year: 2019
Seating, container, children's play element

Beetween was created following a concrete request from QuasiLocanda, located in Villapizzone (MI). It was therefore started by investigating and knowing the reality where the would have been hosted. Since this is a place of encounter, cooperation and conviviality, we arrived at the definition of a hexagonal seat, inspired by the world of bees, especially the shape of the beehive. especially the shape of the beehive. It was not limited to designing simple seats, but something that could also be an element of play for children. The seat is currently located at the venue.

Team: Yaroslava Chepick, Marta Monti, Marco Pizzi, Michele Zanchi

Project: Designing a multifaceted children's chair for a venue

Area: Product
