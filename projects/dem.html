Toy Design

Dem







  • Area: Product
  • Year: 2019
A game designed for nursery schools to encourage movement in children

During the research phase it was found that there is a great need, especially in infancy, to encourage the movement of all the limbs of the body in children, not making the experience of movement something purely gymnastic, but making it fun and stimulating. Dem therefore wants to be a game that invites children to move, especially with the upper limbs of the body (little used), in company. The need for contact, whether with an adult or with a child, is very urgent in children. They need to feel preferred and valued, to feel important. Dem is designed for two children to play together, but it clearly moves even if used by one, going faster. The game is primarily intended for kindergartens and nursery schools, where the dynamics of play between children are favored and where growing together is the order of the day.
